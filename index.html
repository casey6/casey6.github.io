Our Engagement Story

We were vacationing in Hollywood, FL for a few days before we left on a cruise to the Bahamas. We went to a nice dinner one night before taking a walk on the boardwalk. After walking for a bit we then decided to turn around and go check out the Observation Tower.

We climbed to the top of the tower and right away Ashley wanted to take pictures because it was gorgeous! Casey was nonchalantly trying to get her to put her phone away so he could propose. She had no idea what was going on and didn't really listen. Once her phone was finally away, Casey started saying romantic things. Let's be honest, neither of us remember exactly what he said because we were both so caught up in the amazing moment. He then got down on one knee and asked Ashley to marry him. She said "Of course!"

About 30 seconds after he proposed, the security guard came driving by in his truck and honked at us to get down. We got to the tower at 7:34 and it was supposed to close at 7:30. Thank God he was running behind schedule!

After we got down, we walked along the beach and took pictures together. It was an amazing night that we will never forget! We are so happy and so excited to get married June 24, 2016!
